Help

Compulsory School Age in England, Wales and Northern Ireland

A person whose sixteenth birthday occurs before the 31st August is of school leaving age.

Compulsory School Age in Scotland

A person whose sixteenth birthday falls between the 1st March and 30th October is of compulsory school age up until the 31st May that year. Pupils aged sixteen on or between 1st October and the last day of February are still of compulsory school age until the start of the Christmas holidays in that school year.
